# Why we stopped reaching for WordPress

After years of plugin updates, slow editors, and surprise security patches, we drew a line.

## The trade we made

Static MDX with a strict content schema. Every SEO field validated at build time.

## The result

Faster pages, lower hosting cost, predictable deploys. The editor is your IDE.
