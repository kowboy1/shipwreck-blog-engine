## Why a blog at all

A blog gives a site a place to publish content that's slower-changing than news but faster-changing than the static pages. Static pages tell visitors what the site IS; blog posts tell them what's happening AROUND the site — events, explainers, updates, opinions.

For {{siteName}} specifically, this means a place to write things that don't fit neatly into the main navigation but are still worth indexing.
